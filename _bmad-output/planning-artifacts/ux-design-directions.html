UX Design Phase

Design Directions

Exploring visual approaches for expense-app

1. The Minimalist Vault

Focused on radical simplicity and white space. Receipt previews are prioritized, with subtle Indigo accents for status. Ideal for daily capture.

2. Data Insights Dashboard

The "Magical Moment" view. High-fidelity emerald and indigo charts provide instant clarity on spending. Professional and empowering.

3. Zero-Friction Review

Side-by-side verification pattern. Minimizes navigational cognitive load for managers. Clear Emerald/Slate actions.
